01 — The World

A world that looks like ours

Earth 5437 looks exactly like the world outside your window. It isn't. Long ago, a single fork in human history split people into two kinds — and most people alive today don't know which kind they are.

Ductors

Those who kept a living connection to the force beneath everything. Rare. Often unaware of what they carry until something wakes it up.

Sapiens

Everyone else — which is most people, in that world and in ours. No connection to the force, no way to perceive it directly.

The force itself

C.A.K.E.

Compounded Adaptive Kinetic Energy

It isn't magic, and it isn't a gift handed down from anywhere. It's closer to gravity — a fundamental rule of how the universe runs, present in every atom, available to anyone still tuned to receive it. It builds on itself. It moves. It shows up differently depending on who's carrying it.

For centuries, the people who lost their connection to it have been trying to take it back from the people who never did.

The wells

Wherever C.A.K.E. concentrates enough to become perceptible, you get a well — a real, physical place where the Connection runs strongest. Some are ancient. Some are new, called into being by communities who simply stayed together long enough. Some have been corrupted by people trying to seize what they can't access honestly.

Detroit has one. Almost nobody who lives there knows it.

02 — Who's Playing

Three powers, one war

Nobody asked Earth 5437 to choose a side. Three forces shape the world anyway — and most people never learn any of their names.

Resistance

House of Esu

The front line of the old traditions. Training, memory, and the quiet, stubborn work of keeping the Connection alive in places the rest of the world has forgotten to look.

Neutral

The Network

Sometimes called the Violet Hand. The deep state of Earth 5437 — gatekeepers, information brokers, diplomats. Everywhere, and nowhere in particular.

Antagonist

The Society of Gates

Centuries of institutional power, built by people who traded their own connection away for control — and have spent generations since trying to buy, steal, or manufacture it back.
